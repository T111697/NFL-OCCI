What is OCCI?

The Offensive Conflict Creation Index (OCCI) is a comprehensive metric that evaluates how effectively NFL offenses create structural challenges for opposing defenses. It analyzes multiple play-by-play factors including:

  • Motion & Tempo: Pre-snap movement and no-huddle usage
  • Formation Complexity: Personnel groupings and alignments
  • Target Depth: Vertical threat and field stretching
  • Play Action & RPO: Deception and option plays
  • Situational Variance: Unpredictability in play calling

Higher OCCI scores indicate offenses that consistently force defenses to make difficult decisions and adjustments.
